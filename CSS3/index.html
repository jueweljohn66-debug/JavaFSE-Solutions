Community Bulletin

Road Closure Notice

Main Street will be closed for resurfacing from Monday to Wednesday next week. Residents are advised to use alternative routes during this period. The council apologises for any inconvenience caused and thanks the community for their patience.

New Library Hours

Starting from the first of next month, the Central Library will extend its opening hours to include Sunday afternoons from 12pm to 5pm. Digital membership registration is also now available online via the council website.

Council Budget Consultation

The council invites all residents to participate in the annual budget consultation survey. Your feedback directly shapes how community funds are allocated for parks, roads, and local services in the coming year.

Youth Sports Registration

Registration is now open for the summer youth football, basketball, and swimming programmes. Spaces are limited, so early registration is encouraged. Subsidised places are available for eligible families.
